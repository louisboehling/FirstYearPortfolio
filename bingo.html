Inability to connect


The current landscape of the US Education system is extremely restrictive. After receiving a high-school or post-secondary degree, most Americans are cut off from future learning opportunities by their early twenties.

Online platforms such as YouTube, Lynda.com, and Skillshare are affordable options for people who want to learn new skills, but these companies are limited in what they can teach and fail to deliver the in person learning experience that students value.
